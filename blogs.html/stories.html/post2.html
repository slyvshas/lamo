🎬 Introduction

Start with a hook 🎣 to grab attention and keep storytelling simple by focusing on key elements.

📖 Example Story

📝 1. Set the scene – Where are you? What’s happening?
💬 2. Introduce dialogue – What are the exact words exchanged?
🎭 3. Show, don’t tell – Avoid summarizing; instead, zoom into the moment.
❤️ 4. Use emotions and actions – Describe reactions and feelings.
