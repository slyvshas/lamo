Objective:

  • Goats: Trap all 4 Tigers so they cannot move.
  • Tigers: Capture at least 5 Goats by jumping over them.

Game Setup:

  • The board consists of 25 intersection points connected by lines.
  • There are 4 Tigers and 20 Goats.
  • All Tigers start on the board, while Goats are placed during gameplay.

Game Phases:

  1. Phase 1: Placing Goats
    • Players take turns. The Goat player places one Goat on any empty intersection point.
    • The Tiger player can move a Tiger to an adjacent point or capture a Goat if possible.
    • This phase continues until all 20 Goats are placed on the board.
  2. Phase 2: Moving Pieces
    • Goats can move to an adjacent empty point along the lines.
    • Tigers can move to an adjacent empty point or capture a Goat by jumping over it to an empty point.
    • Captures are only valid if the point behind the Goat is empty.

Winning Conditions:

  • Goats win: If all 4 Tigers are trapped and cannot make a valid move.
  • Tigers win: If they capture at least 5 Goats.
